## Why MacPacker?

MacPacker was created to fill a gap in the macOS ecosystem. While Apple's built-in Archive Utility handles basic ZIP files, Mac users working with RAR, 7z, and other modern archive formats needed a better solution.

MacPacker is:
- **Free and Open Source** ‚Äî No hidden costs, no limitations, and full transparency
